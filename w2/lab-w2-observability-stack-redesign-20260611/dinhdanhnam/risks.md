# Risk Register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Loki cannot meet incident-query latency under real production cardinality. | Medium | High | Run the A7 POC in week 1: replay one week of dual-written logs, convert the top 20 historical incident searches to LogQL, and require p95 query latency under 8 seconds over a 24-hour window before week 4 cutover. If it fails, reduce hot retention to 10 days and move high-volume debug namespaces to S3-only. Loki retention/compactor behavior must follow Grafana docs: https://grafana.com/docs/loki/latest/operations/storage/retention/ | Platform observability lead |
| Tail-sampling policy misses rare payment or checkout traces. | Medium | High | Start with conservative OTel tail-sampling policies: keep 100% of errors, timeouts, and p95+ traces for `payment-svc`, `checkout-svc`, and dependencies; review missed-trace drills weekly before retiring Datadog APM. Use OTel sampling guidance and the Collector tail-sampling processor docs as the policy source: https://opentelemetry.io/docs/concepts/sampling/ and https://github.com/open-telemetry/opentelemetry-collector-contrib/blob/main/processor/tailsamplingprocessor/README.md | Payments tech lead |
| Alert migration semantic drift causes missed or duplicate pages. | Medium | High | Require 95% critical/high alert parity, seven-day shadow evaluation against Datadog/Splunk monitors, and two synthetic cascade tests that each create exactly one grouped incident before IRM becomes primary. Keep PagerDuty receiver configured for 30 days after cutover. | Incident response owner |
| OSS operational burden is underestimated. | Medium | Medium | Keep the explicit `0.5 FTE = $9,310/month` cost-model line, assign weekly upgrade/capacity ownership, and define observability-stack SLOs for Grafana, Loki, Tempo, VictoriaMetrics, and Alertmanager. If toil exceeds 8 hours/week for two consecutive weeks, pause vendor decommission and fund managed support or reduce retention. | Platform manager |
| Splunk notice window or 100 GB/day export cap delays vendor exit. | High | Medium | Put the 90-day non-renewal deadline in IRM and team calendars in week 1; export audit data immediately after S3/Athena report parity passes, throttled below the 100 GB/day contract cap; keep Splunk read-only until legal confirms non-renewal. | Engineering operations |
| Metric cardinality regression recreates SaaS-style overage or overloads VictoriaMetrics. | Medium | Medium | Add CI linting for metric labels, Alloy/vmagent denylist rules for `customer_id`, `session_id`, request IDs, and other dynamic labels, plus a weekly top-cardinality report to service owners. vmagent relabeling is the enforcement point: https://docs.victoriametrics.com/victoriametrics/vmagent/ | Service team leads |
